Cowboy Racer: Mesa Run

Rescue Elena from Blackjack Burke and his Desert Vipers.

Hero: Jesse Quill, a quick-witted trail medic turned rider. His mare Satchel runs like a dust devil.

Villain: Blackjack Burke, gambler-turned-bandit with a clockwork cuirass. He and his Vipers clog the road with ambushes.

Damsel: Elena Alvarado, a frontier botanist who mapped hidden springs.

Goal: Cross the desert, crack the Vipers with your whip, and duel Blackjack at the canyon gate.

How to Play

  • Steer with ← → (or tap Left/Right). Adjust speed with ↑ ↓.
  • Use Space (or tap WHIP) to lash nearby road villains. Cooldown applies.
  • Avoid collisions; they chip your health. Smack foes to clear the way and gain score.
  • Ride the distance bar to the end to reach the Boss duel. Whip when his weak core flashes.
